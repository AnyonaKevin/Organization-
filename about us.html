Background

Our Child support organization is a non-profit organizations that provide legal and financial assistance to parents who are trying to collect child support from the non-custodial parent. Our organization has been in operation for a couple of years since 2019 upto date. It has been able to help parents with valuable resource for them to collect child support and guidance during a difficult time especially this difficult economic times. We also work closely with NGOs that support us in all ways and the goverment to enable families thrive in one way or the other.

MISSION

  1. Establish paternity
  2. Obtain child support orders
  3. Enforce child support orders
  4. Promote emotional involvement of both parents
  5. Collaborate with state agencies and community organizations
